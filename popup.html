ChatGPT Folderizr: Organize Conversations


Introduction

This script, ChatGPT Folderizr, organizes your conversations into folders within the DOM. Conversations will be moved based on naming conventions. To use this feature, simply toggle the "Enable/Disable" button. You can also rename conversations to specify a folder assignment by enclosing the folder name in square brackets.

Architectural Choices

The activate/deactivate functionality is stored locally to remember your choice. This approach aligns well with the underlying architecture of ChatGPT, preserving transparency of the pre-existing features. Folders are generated dynamically based on the names of the conversations, offering you great flexibility to manage your chats.

Important Note on Renaming and Deleting

Due to the architectural choices of ChatGPT Folderizr, renaming or deleting conversations while the tool is active could lead to unpredictable behavior. To avoid issues, please disable ChatGPT Folderizr using the "Enable/Disable" button before making any name changes or deletions.
